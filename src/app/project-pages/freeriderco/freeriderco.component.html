Freerider Co.

The brief

Create engaging social media animations and online advertisements for a maternity lifestyle brand. Promote their sustainable materials and being a relatable brand for new parents. Work alongside the founder, helping with content ideas and bringing motion to the products to increase engagement and brand exposure.

The approach

From instagram branding to email campaigns, I bring motion to the brand photography through playful gifs. Creating brand stickers that can be used on instagram, which through GIPHY, have reached over 1.5 million views. Helping to make consistent but playful brand guidelines for social media, including graphic elements and colour palettes.

The process

Aiming to boost the brands recognisability, by posting consistently with the brand elements subtly included throughout. Matching and improving the graphics with the already established brand style.
