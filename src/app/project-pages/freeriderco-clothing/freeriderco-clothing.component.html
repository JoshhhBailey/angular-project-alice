Freerider Co. Clothing Launch

The brief

Freerider are launching their new maternity clothing range, branching out from baby carriers and changing bags. This involved new brand photography, a social media launch campaign and a new website design. We needed to change the brand identity to match with new brand colours and a clean visual style, with a bit more edge.

The solution

We created suspense with teasers and behind the scenes footage of the shoot. With the tag line ‘The Maternity Revolution’ to mark our brand change and how Freerider intend to change the Maternity clothing market. This also involved detailed previews of each product, highlighting the key benefits such as the sustainable materials.

Also taking advantage of reels to engage and drive views towards the brand, in the anticipation to launch. The colour scheme was adapted to have more earthy, natural tones to reflect the materials and environmental impact of the brand.
