Soy & Melt Candle Co.

The brief

Soy and Melt is a new candle company that uses up-cycled containers, such as jars and cut Prosecco bottles. The brand identity needed to reflect the sustainable elements with modern and simple style. The brand product photography also needs to reflect the values and showcase the scents visually.

The goal

To build the brand presence on social media to produce traffic to their instagram shop and also their Etsy site. The competition of other up-cycled candles, tend to lack strong visual presence so this is where Soy and Melt will begin to stand out with their distinct and consistent modern branding. The brand also aims to promote sustainable messages and encourage their audience to reuse and recycle.
