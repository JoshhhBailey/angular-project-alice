The brief

Whilst working at The Sideshow Agency, I had the opportunity to storyboard and design assets for an EE branded animation. We needed to launch EE’s new plans with an explainer ('in a nutshell’) type video for their internal sales team. This involved creating vector illustrations in the EE brand style and telling a story alongside the script.

The approach

Setting the scene by highlighting key aspects and benefits using simple illustrations. The EE brand colours are aqua, yellow and grey, which remain consistent throughout the design.
